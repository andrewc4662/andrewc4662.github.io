Awarness with phaser

Using good for good!

I mainly created this game with the intent to help spread awarness on pollution as i felt that is wasnt really talked about as much as it was in years prior. I also built off the idea that with more knowledge, people could take more measures to help save the planet, so what wouldnt be better than doing that with a game?. Phaser is a javascript library and with its capabilities I was able to make a jumper game where you collect "polluted" air in orde to help save the world but as you wipe out pollution a new threat appears which can end your game. I really like what I ended up with and with the new knowledge of phaser i plan to bring my sep9 freedom project creation to life in phaseras an air purifier could be made in phaser as it would basically be an animation, so stay tuned for that in the near future.
